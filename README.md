Drunker's_Helper
=============

## Overview
An Android application to count beers track location and share with your best friends.

##  Features
1. Choose what beer are you drinking and start counting.
2. By enable GPS and internet connection you can add your location and what you are drinking.
3. Share what and where you are drinking with your best friends on Facebook, Twitter, mail, etc….
4. If you don’t find the beer that you are drinking you can add new ones.
5. Keep the historic and tracking the places where you drink beers.
